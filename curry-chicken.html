Curry Chicken Recipe

Description

Perfectly spicy, tender, and drenched in a beautifully thick curry sauce that's sure to be a crowd-pleaser. Served with Rice and Peas.

Ingredients

  • Pimento
  • Curry powder
  • Scallions
  • Carrots
  • Thyme
  • Ginger
  • Scotch bonnet
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • White potato
  • Chicken leg & thighs

Steps

  1. Add the spices to the chicken thighs, and cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour or up to overnight.
  2. Once the chicken is done marinating, remove the onions, peppers, thyme, scotch bonnet pepper and garlic cloves from chicken and set aside.
  3. Heat oil in a large heavy bottom pot over medium heat
  4. Add onions, green pepper, scotch bonnet peppers, garlic cloves, and thyme to the pot and stir until the onions have softened
  5. Add chicken pieces and Â¼ cup of water to the pot. Cover and reduce heat to medium-low. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 30-35 minutes.
  6. When there are about 15 minutes remaining, stir in the potato and continue to cook covered.
  7. Once the chicken is cooked and potatoes are softened, remove from heat. Salt and pepper to taste.
